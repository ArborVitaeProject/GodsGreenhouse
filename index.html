Welcome to God's Greenhouses

Our mission is to create sustainable, community-driven greenhouses that bring people closer to nature and each other.

Our Mission

We aim to provide resources and knowledge to create a network of interconnected communities rooted in shared values and beliefs. We encourage homesteading, combining church, farming, school, and construction to support self-sustaining and thriving communities.

Our Values

  • Spiritual Growth: Focus on personal spiritual development and willingness to cooperate with God's will.
  • Humility: Remain humble and seek guidance from spiritual mentors.
  • Compassion: Show compassion and understanding to others in their spiritual journey.
  • Forgiveness: View others with charity and forgiveness.
  • Obedience: Place hope in God and be obedient in fulfilling His will.

Join Our Community

Become a part of our initiative to create a sustainable future. Volunteer, donate, or simply spread the word.

Educational Programs

Our educational programs are designed to teach practical skills and knowledge about sustainable living, farming, and community building. We focus on hands-on learning experiences that can be applied in real-world scenarios.

Greenhouse Initiative

Our Greenhouse Initiative aims to create local greenhouses in communities to provide fresh, natural produce. This initiative is part of our broader goal to return to a more natural way of living, inspired by the Garden of Eden. We believe that by working together, we can create a self-sustaining environment that nourishes both the body and the soul.
